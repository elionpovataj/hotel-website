About Kosovo

Kosovo is a small, landlocked country in Southeast Europe, located in the central Balkans. Its capital, Pristina, is known for its vibrant café culture, youthful energy, and rich history. Kosovo declared independence in 2008 and continues to grow as a modern European destination.

Kosovo’s Culture

Tradition & Folklore

Kosovo’s traditional music, dance, and costumes reflect its deep roots in Albanian and Balkan culture. Festivals often feature folk songs, dancing, and colorful attire.

Art & Modern Identity

Contemporary art and film have thrived in Kosovo. The annual Dokufest in Prizren is one of Southeast Europe’s most famous documentary film festivals.
